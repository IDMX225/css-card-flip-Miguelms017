A Colombian Recipe

This colombian recipe in a popular dish not only in colombia, but also worldwide. It's name is Pan de bono. this traditional bread from Valle del Cauca in Colombia is one of the classical traditional dishes from the colombian culture. Try it! I know you're going to love it.

Pan De Bono

Pan De Bono

Preheat the furnace at 400F, then, mix the corn and yuca flour until all is completely mixed, then add an egg and mix until have a dough. Then, divide the dough in 12 parts and form balls. Put the divided doug in a furnace tray and bake for 15 or 20 minutes or until become gold. serve hot
